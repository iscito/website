I have been on the Project Management Committee for the Apache PLC4X project for the past few years. I am also interested in integrating streaming platforms, Kafka, MQTT, etc... with SCADA systems to be able to provide advanced solutions within the preventative maintenance, reporting and distributed computing spaces.
